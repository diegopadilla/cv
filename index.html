I am a professor at Cornell University. My research focuses on issues at the interface of networks and information, with an emphasis on the social and information networks than underpin the Web and the other on-line media. My work has been supported by an NSF Career Award, an ONR Young Investigator Award, and grants from Facebook, Google, the ARO, and the NSF.
